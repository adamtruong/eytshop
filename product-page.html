While there is a milestone to honor — and a new section in the book on Why Milestones Are Important — maintaining the original content demonstrates that this book has genuinely weathered the test of time. The book's sidebars (which were updated for the 20th anniversary edition) have been updated again, but the core principles that parents and grandparents embraced 25 years ago are now being shared with new generations who have discovered that its timeless wisdom and no-nonsense lessons can be applied to anyone's life and their vision for a future that includes taking control of their finances.
Milestones are celebrated by people from many cultures and regions. We use them to track time, track progress, reflect on lessons gained, and celebrate achievements... They provide purpose to our life's journey. They are a means for us to merge the past, present, and future... looking back at where we started, where we are now, and the hope of what the future can bring.
So much has changed in our world in the quarter century since Rich Dad Poor Dad was originally published - 25 years since April 8, 1997. But one thing has remained constant: the critical need of financial education. Whether we like it or not, money is still an important part of our lives, and technology has brought both speed and creativity to the realm of money. In an ever-changing environment, we can all grow smarter about money... and learn everything we can to ensure our future.


Author  Robert T. Kiyosaki
